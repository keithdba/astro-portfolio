subtitle: "A Multi‑Layer Human → Copilot → Antigravity Workflow"
overview: "Vibe Coding is an emerging approach to product creation that blends human intent, AI‑assisted reasoning, and adaptive execution environments. Instead of treating design, development, and iteration as separate phases, Vibe Coding unifies them into a continuous, conversational workflow. The goal is simple: reduce cognitive load, eliminate brittle orchestration, and let humans operate at the level of intent while AI systems translate that intent into structured artifacts, prototypes, and working experiences. This approach has shaped the early projects in this space — IdeaGen, VibeGen, and the macdaly.com site itself — each serving as a proving ground for how AI‑native workflows can accelerate creation without locking into rigid stacks or manual pipelines."
